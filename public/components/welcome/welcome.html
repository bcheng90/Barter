About Barter

It started with an question. A thought that if we share our talents and our time, how much can we learn? Barter was started for the sole purpose of taking the expenses of having a tutor and replacing it with giving your time to teach one another your talents. A community built by offering what you know to the world. A community built to connect people globally through your knowledge.

Teach, grow, learn.
